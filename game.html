The buttons below let you place different objects onto the map. You can either click or click and drag to place. Roads (blue) are faster to travel on, mountains (light grey) are slower to travel on, and walls (dark grey) can't be traveled on. Click the radio buttons to change the heuristic used by the algorithm. For the roads/mountains to properly work, the heuristic must be set to something other than Manhattan. Click the Play button to actually start the algorithm. Clicking on the map while playing will move the player (green). The numbers in each cell represent the cost of moving to that cell. The path will be displayed in blue.
